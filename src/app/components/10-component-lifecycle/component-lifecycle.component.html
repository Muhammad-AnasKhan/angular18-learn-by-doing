Component Lifecycle Hooks

A component's lifecycle is the sequene of steps that happen between the component's creation and its destruction.
1. constructor()
2. {{'@'}}OnInit()
3. {{'@'}}OnChanges()
4. {{'@'}}DoCheck()
5. {{'@'}}AfterContentInit()
6. {{'@'}}AfterContentChecked()
7. {{'@'}}AfterViewInit()
8. {{'@'}}AfterViewChecked()
9. {{'@'}}OnDestroy()
