Guards

Guards are a way to control access to routes in an Angular application.
There are four types of guards:
- CanActivate: Prevents access to a route until a certain condition is met.
- CanActivateChild: Prevents access to child routes until a certain condition is met.
- CanDeactivate: Prevents navigation away from a route until a certain condition is met.
- CanLoad: Prevents lazy loading of a route until a certain condition is met.
Guards are implemented as arrow functions that implement the CanActivate, CanActivateChild, CanDeactivate, and CanLoad interfaces.
